How does OnlyFans work?

OnlyFans' core business is to create a privileged link between "creators" and their subscribers. The site offers its users to subscribe to profiles of personalities, in order to have access to exclusive content (videos, photos, private messaging) that are not available anywhere else on the internet. While some profiles offer free content, the vast majority of content producers offer subscriptions ranging from $4.99 to $49.99 per month (about $4 to $43), with a sliding scale of charges depending on the length of the subscription. Fans" can also tip in exchange for a dedication or personalized content.

Is OnlyFans illegal?

Fans and creators must be 18 years old, and not include anyone under that age in their content. They must only use their accounts for a lawful purpose. The acceptable use information in the OnlyFans terms of service sets out clearly what is allowed on the platform, and more importantly, what isn't.


Anyone can create a "creator" account on the platform, provided they are of age and have a bank account, if they wish to charge for access to their publications. Content producers most often invite their subscribers to join them on OnlyFans from other mainstream sites or social networks, such as Twitter or Instagram. After five years of existence, OnlyFans claims 130 million users worldwide.
